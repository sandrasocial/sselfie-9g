## Reel Best Practices:

**Hook Formula:**
- First 3 seconds = Problem or surprising statement
- Must stop the scroll immediately

**Text Overlays:**
- Large, readable font
- Contrast with background
- Key points only (not full script)

**Trending Audio:**
- Use trending sounds in your niche
- Match audio vibe to content vibe
- Original audio works too if it's catchy

**CTA:**
- Always end with "Link in bio"
- Use arrow pointing up
- Make it clear and simple

**Posting:**
- Post 2-3 reels per week
- Best times: 9 AM, 12 PM, 6 PM
- Use trending sounds when possible
- Repurpose top-performing reels
