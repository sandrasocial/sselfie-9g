## Repeat & Optimize

After 30 days, analyze:

**Check Analytics:**
- Which posts got most saves?
- Which reels got most shares?
- Which stories got most link clicks?
- What time of day performs best?

**Optimize:**
- Do more of what works
- Iterate on what doesn't
- Test new templates
- Adjust posting times

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Value (educational)
- 30% Behind-the-Scenes (humanize)
- 20% Transformation (social proof)
- 10% Community (engagement)

**Link in Bio Clicks Goal:**
- Week 1: 200 clicks
- Week 2: 250 clicks
- Week 3: 300 clicks
- Week 4: 350 clicks

**Follower Growth Goal:**
- Week 1: +125 followers
- Week 2: +150 followers
- Week 3: +175 followers
- Week 4: +200 followers
