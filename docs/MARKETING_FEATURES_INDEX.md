## üìã Executive Summary

**SSELFIE Studio** is an AI-powered personal brand photography platform that helps women entrepreneurs create professional brand photos every month without a photographer. The platform combines AI image generation, Instagram strategy, content planning, and educational resources into one comprehensive solution.

**Target Audience:** Women entrepreneurs, content creators, personal brand builders, coaches, consultants, and lifestyle influencers who need consistent, professional brand photos for Instagram and their business.

**Core Value Proposition:** "100 professional brand photos every month for less than the price of a coffee a day ($97/month)"

## üéØ USER-FACING FEATURES

### 1. STUDIO (Model Training)

**Purpose:** Users upload photos to train their personal AI model

**Features:**
- Upload 10-20 selfies to build personal AI model
- View training status and model readiness
- Manage training photos
- Retrain model option
- Training costs 25 credits
- Model training takes ~2 hours

**User Journey:**
1. First-time users see onboarding wizard
2. Upload training photos
3. Wait for model training completion
4. Receive notification when ready

**Marketing Angle:** "Train your AI model once, get unlimited photos forever"

---

### 2. MAYA - AI Stylist & Chat Interface

**Purpose:** AI-powered stylist that helps users create styled photoshoots through conversation

**Key Capabilities:**
- **Classic Mode:** Uses Flux LoRA models with trigger words
- **Pro Mode:** Advanced image generation with identity preservation
- **Concept Generation:** Creates styled shoot concepts based on brand profile
- **Real-time Fashion Research:** Web search integration for current trends (2025)
- **Brand-Aware Styling:** Understands user's brand vibe, color palette, and aesthetic
- **Multiple Tabs:**
  - Chat: Main conversation interface
  - Concepts: Quick inspiration cards
  - Feed: Instagram feed planning integration
  - Videos: B-roll and video content generation
  - Prompts: Quick prompt suggestions
  - Training: Model training status

**Maya's Expertise:**
- Scandinavian Minimalism
- Dark Moody Aesthetics
- Editorial Fashion Photography
- Personal Brand Storytelling
- Luxury influencer content with 2026 trends
