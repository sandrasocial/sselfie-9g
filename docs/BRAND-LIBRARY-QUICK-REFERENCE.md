## üìä BRAND MIX GUIDELINES

### Athletic Outfits
- **3-4 branded pieces:** Top, bottom, shoes, bag
- **0-1 unbranded:** Props only

### Casual Outfits
- **2-3 branded pieces:** Jeans, shoes, maybe bag
- **2-3 unbranded:** Top, cap, accessories

### Street Style
- **2-3 branded pieces:** Jeans, shoes, 1 luxury bag
- **3-4 unbranded:** Outerwear, top, accessories, jewelry

### Travel
- **3-4 branded pieces:** Top, bottom, bag, luggage
- **1 unbranded:** Shoes (sometimes)

### Cozy
- **1-2 branded pieces:** Slippers, maybe 1 luxury accessory
- **3-4 unbranded:** Sweater, joggers, accessories

### Luxury
- **1-2 luxury pieces:** Bag, maybe jewelry
- **3-4 unbranded:** Basics (sweater, pants, etc.)
